Autonomy Hall

Auotonomy Hall is the latest addition to the Halls of Residence on the College of Technology Education – Kumasi Campus of the University. The Hall derives its name from the fact that it is the first Hall to be built after the University gained autonomy in May 2004. The Autonomy Hall is a mixed hall with one hundred and sixty-five (165) rooms and houses nearly 700 residents. Construction works on the government-sponsored Autonomy Hall were completed in 2012 and inaugurated on June 22, 2013.18 Jul 2021 Situated on a hill and away from the main classroom block, the Hall accommodates five hundred and ninety-six (596) students, making it the largest Hall to date on the Kumasi Campus. Built with student relaxation and recreation in mind, the Hall provides services such as a Junior Common Room, a restaurant, internet café, super market as well as a computer room. Not only does the Hall’s architecture seek to provide respite from the hustle and bustle of campus life, it also takes into consideration students’ desire for a conducive learning environment which is evident in the Hall’s three (3) study rooms. Hall management is top-notch and committed to providing the best experience that a student of UEW could possibly hope for. Contact: Estate Officer, Mr. Osei Akoto Baffour. Phone: 03220 52236.

Opoku Ware II Hall

Atwima Hall is also a mixed hall located at the Kumasi Campus of the College of Technology Education, University of Education, Winneba. It comprises two blocks, namely ‘A’ and ‘B’. Block ‘A’ accommodates 70 male students whilst block ‘B’ accommodates 100 female students. The hall has a professional team that manages students’ accommodation through the provision of quality services. Enjoy a serene learning atmosphere from the gardens and flora background that add great beauty to the hall. Other facilities Atwima can boast of include a well-furnished-air-conditioned 150-sitting-capacity reading room, a multipurpose sports court for volleyball, badminton and basketball as well as a well-furnished Junior Common Room with a wireless internet facility that is accessed free of charge. Students also enjoy a 24-hour – DSTV service. In addition, the Hall has a shopping mall and a restaurant attached to it. Contact: Hall Manager, Mr. Richard Mprah Phone: 0244703214/03220 53603.

Atwima Hall

UEW Opoku Ware II HallOpoku ware II Hall is one of the Halls of Residence on the Kumasi Campus of the College of Technology Education, University of Education, Winneba. It has five blocks namely, A, B, C, D and E. Blocks A, B and D accommodate males whilst Block C and E accommodate females. It has a Five Hundred and Fifty (550) Student capacity. The beauty of the new extension block fitted with the state of the arts facilities makes the hall attractive and magnificent. The rooms are specious with comfortable beds. The frontage and the middle of the hall are spread with special greenery of flowers giving beautiful scenery which makes the occupants feel comfortable all the times. Our special facilities include air conditioned JCR and study rooms, internet café and bore hole fitted with pumps for constant supply of water. DSTV facility is also available. You can also access the internet in your room. The hall is managed by a professional management team which is sensitive to the needs of the student body and strives hard to make the students’ academic dreams come true. Contact: Hall Manager: Mr. Richard Mprah. Phone: 0244703214/03220 53603.
